ILLUSTRATION PROJECT

I won an illustration contest named "Il Calendario del Noce", launched by a local foster home as a charity event. The goal was to create an illustration based on the afrocan proverb "it takes a village to raise a child". I've used this situation as an opportunity to show my skill with vector illustration!

View JPEG Illustration
1 - First approach

I've started my project thinking about all the key words related to the proverb: education, growth, child, love, tenderness, knowledge, ideas, development. After this brief, i've decided to which words where the most important that i wanted to include into the drawing.

2 - Brainstorming

The first thing that came up in my mind, after i've read the proverb, was the image of a child surrounded by house and different structures with the presence of elements that might indicate every experience the enviroment in which you grow up can give and shape you.

3 - Composition

My main goal was to create an image rich of symbols which make it easy and intuitive to understand. Uniform and balanced, where all the elements are connected to each other might indicate every experience the enviroment in which you grow up can give and shape you.

The child goes at the center of the artboard, holded by a hand as protection, but also as a better way to show th tenderness and vulnerability of a new life. All the other elements like the city/village and the grass, are placed in the background as way to wrap everything around the new life, but also creating a uniform appearance.

As you can see in the sky, there are icons. Thinking about the african proverb...what an entire village can give to a child to raise? Well, everything. 4 - Final thoughs

I have choosen to create this piece with vector design since is one of my aquired skills and it was useful to take this risk and speriment with my knowledge!
